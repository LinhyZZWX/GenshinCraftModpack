## **GenshinCraft Modpack Description**  
### **Main Purpose:**  
To provide gameplay, visuals, fonts, and other content close to those of Genshin Impact. Fun yet smooth.  

### **Performance Improvements:**  
Includes several excellent optimization mods to enhance game performance, making it significantly smoother than the vanilla version. Specific effects may vary depending on the renderer, hardware, etc., and optimization is usually more noticeable on lower-performance devices. (P.S.: Genshin Impact itself is quite well-optimized, by the way.)  

### **Gameplay Similarity:**  
Recreates the gameplay of Genshin Impact through the mod "Genshin Craft", offering an excellent experience for Genshin players.  

### **Interface Optimization:**  
Simplifies the vanilla game UI based on "Fancy Menu" and "Drippy Loading Screen," removing unnecessary features such as NCR options and Realms. Additionally, "CozyUI" and its associated resource packs are incorporated to beautify the vanilla UI.  
⚠ Warning: May cause abnormal game interface. Please play at 4x interface size.  

*Enjoy it!*

## **GenshinCraft 整合包说明**  
### **主要目的：**  
提供接近《原神》的玩法、画面、字体等内容，好玩但不失流畅。  

### **性能提升：**  
加入了一些优秀的优化模组来提升游戏性能，可以比原版流畅不少。具体效果因渲染器、硬件等而异，通常在低性能设备上优化效果更明显。（附注：话说《原神》本身的优化挺不错的。）  

### **玩法接近：**  
通过模组“Genshin Craft”还原《原神》的玩法，为《原神》玩家提供优秀体验。  

### **界面优化：**  
基于“Fancy Menu”和“Drippy Loading Screen”对游戏原版 UI 进行简化，去除了不必要的功能，例如 NCR 选项、Realms。同时加入“CozyUI”及其附属资源包来美化原版 UI。  
**⚠ 警告：可能导致游戏界面异常，请在 4x 界面大小下游玩。**  

*开始游玩吧！*
